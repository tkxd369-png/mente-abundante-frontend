The Law of Life
Phrase of the day

Life does not need permission to exist. It moves from fullness to fullness.

Focus of the day

Everything alive unfolds toward growth. Life itself carries provision, direction, and purpose. You are not chasing life — you are stewarding it. True abundance begins by recognizing that life within you already contains design, movement, and potential.

Inner Reflection

  • Where do I resist natural growth?
  • Where am I forcing instead of aligning?
  • What part of me feels alive right now?
  • What does fullness mean beyond possessions?
