Cycle 2 · Day 07
Life Is a Gift
Phrase of the day

Life is not something you chase.
Life is something already alive within you.

Focus of the day

Everything that has life moves forward — not because it is pushed, but because movement is its nature.

Life does not begin in lack. It begins whole. And from that wholeness it unfolds.

A seed does not doubt growth. A river does not doubt arrival. Life aligns with what it is.

Life is already given.
What has life flows.
Fullness moves toward expression.

The belief that something is missing is learned — not real.

You are not empty. You are already in motion.

Inner Reflection

  • Where have I believed life was incomplete?
  • Where do I feel life already moving?

Journal

  1. Life is moving in me through…
  2. Today I allow life by…

Closing

Life unfolds when trusted.
