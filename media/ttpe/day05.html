Focus of the day

Today we do not add new concepts. Today we stop—and we gather what has already touched you. Because transformation is not information. Transformation is what you keep, what you practice, and what you protect.

Go back through Days 01–04. Move slowly. Underline what pierced through the noise. Circle what brought you peace. Mark what exposed a lie that has been shaping you. This is your inner work—private, honest, sacred.
